Welcome to Shankar Label

Located in the heart of Tirupur, Tamil Nadu, India's textile city, Shankar Label has been a leading player in the industry since its inception in 2000. Founded with a vision to provide high-quality labeling solutions, our company has grown significantly over the years, with a current turnover of ₹20 crores per annum.

Our Journey

Starting with an investment of ₹3 crores, we have consistently worked towards innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our dedication to excellence has enabled us to build a strong reputation in the market, and we continue to strive for growth and improvement.

Our Leadership

Under the guidance of our Managing Director, Mr. Shankar, Shankar Label has achieved remarkable success. His leadership and expertise have been instrumental in shaping our company's vision and strategy.

Our Commitment

At Shankar Label, we are committed to delivering exceptional quality products and services that meet the evolving needs of our customers. Our state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology enable us to stay ahead of the curve and provide innovative solutions to the textile industry.

Our Values

Quality: We prioritize quality in every aspect of our business, from production to customer service. Innovation: We continuously strive to innovate and improve our products and processes. Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the forefront of everything we do, and we aim to exceed their expectations.
