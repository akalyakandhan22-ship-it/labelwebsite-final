Dtf stickers

DTF stickers (Direct to Film stickers) are a type of high-quality, durable stickers made using the direct-to-film printing method. They allow for vibrant, full-color, and detailed printing with strong adhesion, usually on a PET or PVC film. These stickers are especially popular for customizing garments but can be used on various surfaces like laptops, water bottles, packaging, and more.

DTF (Direct-to-Film) stickers are made by printing designs onto a special PET film using DTF inks and adhesive powder, then heat pressing them onto different surfaces. They are durable, waterproof, flexible, and vibrant, making them suitable for both light and dark materials. Unlike vinyl, they don’t require cutting or weeding, which saves time and effort. DTF stickers can be applied on fabric, glass, plastic, wood, metal, leather, and paper, making them highly versatile.
