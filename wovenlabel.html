Woven Labels

Shankar labels company specializes in designing and manufacturing labels for garments, accessories, and textiles, with the goal of enhancing brand identity and product quality. These labels are produced through a weaving process, combining warp and weft yarns on looms to create textile and durable labels that display logos, brand names, care instructions, and other details. Woven labels stand out for their longevity and resistance to fading, maintaining the brand's signature throughout the life of a garment .
woven label companies offer a variety of label designs and finishes—including Damask, Satin, Taffeta, and cotton options—with custom folds such as end fold, loop fold, and center fold. The development process typically starts with understanding the customer's design and functional requirements, moves to artwork and sample creation, and ends with bulk production that ensures consistency and premium quality. Companies often provide eco-friendly and high-definition weave options to meet market trends and brand sustainability commitments. The woven labels produced not only convey brand and product information but also serve as a sutable form of advertising, adding a touch of professionalism and luxury to fashion and textile products.

Woven labels are premium, durable, and luxurious tags made by weaving threads (like polyester or cotton) together on a loom, resulting in delicate designs directly into the fabric for a high-quality, long-lasting finish.
