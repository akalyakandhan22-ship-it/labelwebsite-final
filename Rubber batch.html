Rubber batches (or patches) for garments are typically made from materials like silicone rubber, PVC, or other flexible rubber compounds. These batches are popular in the garment industry for attaching branding, logos, or embellishments because they are durable, water-resistant, and flexible. They can be molded into various shapes and colors and customized with 2D or 3D designs

rubber batch is a measured mixture of raw rubber with specific chemicals such as fillers, accelerators, activators, plasticizers, and curing agents, prepared for processing in rubber manufacturing. It ensures uniform properties, consistency, and quality in the final product. Rubber batches are processed through mixing machines like two-roll mills or internal mixers, which evenly distribute the ingredients.
