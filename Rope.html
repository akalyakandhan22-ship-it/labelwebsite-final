Cotton rope is a natural fiber rope made from 100% cotton, known for being soft, lightweight, and eco-friendly. It is often used in crafting, DIY projects, home décor, and industrial applications. Cotton ropes come in various types such as twisted, braided, and multi-ply, with diameters ranging from very thin (1 mm) to thick sizes over 25 mm.

Cotton rope is a strong and flexible rope made from natural cotton fibers, known for its soft texture, good grip, and eco-friendliness. It is biodegradable, durable, washable, and non-irritating to skin, making it safe for daily use.
